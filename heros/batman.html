BATMAN

About Batman

Batman, aka Bruce Wayne, is a billionaire philanthropist by day and Gotham’s protector by night. With no superpowers, he uses intelligence, gadgets, martial arts, and fear tactics to fight crime.

Fun Facts

  • First appeared in 1939.
  • Member of the Justice League.
  • Uses the Batmobile, Batarangs, and a utility belt full of gadgets.
